Programs and Activities

Free Civil Service Examination Review

This free Civil Service Exam Review aims to ensure a generation of competent civil service professionals, preparing Camarines Norte youth to become productive citizens with employable skills and proficiency. The activity aims to equip Camarines Norte youth with the necessary knowledge and competency to pass the Civil Service Eligibility exams, ensuring a future generation of competent civil service professionals, thereby promoting productivity and employability.

Sangguniang Kabataan Mandatory Training

This vital program equips youth leaders with essential skills for effective governance and community service. Through workshops and modules, participants learn about local governance, youth development, legal frameworks, and ethical responsibilities. This training empowers officials to initiate community projects, promote youth participation, and advocate for local youth welfare, fostering competent leadership and community progress.

Parada ng Kabataan Kontra Droga

Barkada Kontra Droga is a peer group that promotes awareness and intervention to drug abuse and related issues. The Barkada Kontra Droga is designed as a preventive education and information program, empowering individuals to advocate for a healthy lifestyle through various activities. The organization aims to evolve into a nationwide Barkadahan concept, strengthening camaraderie, collaboration, cooperation, solidarity, and linkages in achieving a drug-free lifestyle.

Bantayog Summer Youth Camp 2024

The Summer Youth Camp aims to promote youth participation in governmental activities, public and civic affairs, and develop their sense of leadership and patriotism. This camp covers topics such as career paths, sustainable development, environment preservation, culture, sports, gender equality, and mental health wellness.

Free Criminologist Licensure Examination Review

The Provincial Youth Development Office is dedicated to enhancing educational and career opportunities for the youth in our community. As part of this commitment, we offers Free Criminologist Licensure Exam Review Program. This program aimed to help individuals prepare thoroughly for the Criminologist Licensure Examination, which is essential for those seeking to advance their careers in criminology.

Free Licensure Examination for Teachers Review

Teaching is a noble profession with numerous responsibilities, including serving as role models, responsible citizens, and inspiring classroom facilitators. The Provincial Government of Camarines Norte focuses on youth empowerment through education by providing free review sessions in a controlled environment to prepare teachers for their licensure examination, both in elementary and secondary levels.

This year's theme, "From Clicks to Progress: Youth Digital Pathways for Sustainable Development," highlights the crucial role of digital innovation in shaping a sustainable future.
